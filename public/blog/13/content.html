Black Fashion Fair and The Estate of Jean-Michel Basquiat Collaboration

by OGOCHUKWU NWODOH

Black Fashion Fair, an organization that uplifts Black people in fashion through various mediums like exhibitions, publications, and clothing collaborations, has come out with a new collection, "Those Who Dress Better," in partnership with The Estate of Jean-Michel Basquiat.

The collaboration features nine prominent Black designers that have created pieces inspired by Basquiat's work. The pieces range from shoes to jewelry to dresses. The collaboration is on display at the Jean-Michel Basquiat: King Pleasure exhibition. Some items from the partnership are also available for purchase.

This line is a testament to the relationship between fashion and art. It speaks to Jean-Michel Basquiat's present and future relevancy. You can learn more about the collection here.
