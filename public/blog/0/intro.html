What is the Black Fashion Finder? An introduction to what this platform is about

by OGOCHUKWU NWODOH

Here’s a quick introduction to the Black Fashion Finder!

What is the Black Fashion Finder?
The Black Fashion Finder is a permanent space to celebrate all Black contribution to fashion. Right now it is trendy for major publications to talk about Black fashion, specifically black designers. However, within a couple months, if not a couple weeks, these publications will go back to mostly posting about white people in fashion. I want to create a platform where Black people in fashion are centered, recognized, and celebrated all the time.

What will the Black Fashion Finder consist of?
The Black fashion finder will have a directory of Black-owned fashion labels and blog posts about various topics in Black fashion. I don’t support a consumerist/black-capitalist agenda, so I want to make sure that imagery and art is centered as much as possible. That being said, I do want Black-owned brands to receive the proper recognition and monetary compensation they deserve, so I will maintain and expand the directory of Black-owned fashion labels.

What was the thought behind the Black Fashion Finder?
There was a time during COVID-19 where I was looking for small clothing businesses to support and I kept coming across all these Black-owned small fashion brands that I had never heard of. Their creations were very unique and I had never seen those types of clothes sold in large retailers. This app was created because I wanted to capture the unique aesthetics of these brands and help tell their stories.

What Black-owned fashion labels will be available on the black fashion finder?
I want ALL Black-owned fashion labels to be featured on the Black Fashion Finder. I will publish another blog post about how a label can request themselves to be added. Any Black-owned fashion label can request to be added as long as they provide the necessary information. I don’t just want celebrity endorsed brands. I want local brands, brands who are are not present on social media, brands that are niche, and more to be represented on this app :)
