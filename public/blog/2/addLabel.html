Contribute to the Vision: How to add a business to the Black Fashion Finder

As a part of our goal of celebrating all Black contribution to fashion, we’ve built in the ability for any Black-owned fashion label to be listed on the Black Fashion Finder.

Here’s a 6 step guide on how you can add your fashion label to the app.

  1. There is a form on the Black Fashion Finder website for requesting a fashion label to be added. You can access the form by 2 ways: 

    • Open the Black Fashion Finder app, click on the 'More' tab, and click on the button 'Add A Fashion Label'. You will be taken to the form.
    • You can access the form on any browser here.

  2. The first part of the form asks for information about who is filling out the form and their email. We would like this information so that we can contact you if we have questions about your submission.

  3. The next part of the form asks for information about the fashion label: the label name, the owner name, mission statement, and an image that will represent the brand on the app. We ask for this information because we want to properly credit you and help tell the story of your brand. Please upload a high quality image with a square aspect ratio.

  4. After, we ask for more detailed information about the items the brand sells. We ask for you to check off the types of items the brand sells and to select up to three tags that describe the brand. This will help people discover the brand. If there is any tag you feel that is missing, feel free to contact us at info@blackfashionfinder.com to add the tag. Please also indicate if the brand is Black-owned or not. We also ask for price information of the brand so that people will be able to find items that fit into their  budget.

  5. In the next section we ask you to upload a high quality picture and some details about an item the brand sells. This will be used so that people understand your brand more.

  6. The final section asks for contact information so that people can find out more about your brand and make purchases!

That's it! Press submit to submit your form and we will make a decision. If you have any questions, concerns, or feedback hit us up on IG or email us here.
