Black Fashion Week: Daily Paper

MEMOIRS OF A PARALLEL UNIVERSE FALL WINTER 2020

The Brand

Daily Paper is an Amsterdam-based menswear and womenswear brand founded in 2012 by Hussein Suleiman, Jefferson Osei, and Abderrahmane Trabsini. The brand originally started out a blog where the three founders would interview prominent people in the streetwear and sneaker industries. It has now grown into a prominent brand that has been worn by the likes of The Migos, Lupita Nyong'o, and Goldlink. The brand is known for its designs that incorporate elements of African and Dutch culture.

The Collection

Daily Paper has presented its FW 20' collection through a brand-created comic book named "Memoirs of a Parallel Universe". The comic book tells the story of four characters: HoH, Nana, Yannayi and Massassi, who live in a dystopian world, where the of the problems of today’s culture are taken to the extreme. Each character in the book has their own personal style which is reflected in the actual garments that are available for purchase. The comic book explores questions related to cultural assimilation, imperialism, social media, and inter-generational family relationships. The clothing collection is a statement on these issues. Aesthetically, the collection itself contains a mix of pieces that contain imagery from the comic book and basics like oversized t-shirts, bodysuits, and hoodies. This mix of styles makes the collection fun, but still sophisticated and functional.
