Black Fashion Week: J. Bolin

THE LUXE COLLECTION & LABOR DAY COLLECTION

The Brand

J. Bolin is the eponymous brand of celebrity stylist Jason Bolin. Bolin has worked with an impressive range of clients that include Vivica A. Fox, Boris Kodjoe, Morris Chestnut, Nene Leakes, Christina Milian, and Niecy Nash. J.Bolin is a size inclusive brand that has the goal of making everyone that wears their clothes feel like a star.

The Collection

The back to back releases of these collections speak to J. Bolin’s creative range. The both collections reflect J. Bolin’s signature look of mixing prints. The Luxe collection features formal, brightly colored statement pieces that experiment with color, print, and texture. The Labor Day Collection features pieces that are also glamourous, but are more for everyday wear.
