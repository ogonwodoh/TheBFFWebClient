Black Fashion Week: Fear of God

THE SEVENTH COLLECTION - Spring 2021 Menswear

The Brand

Fear of God is a streetwear brand founded in 2013 by Jerry Lorenzo. The brand’s aesthetic is influenced by Lorenzo’s childhood memories playing baseball and 90s pop culture symbols like Kurt Cobain. Lorenzo has collaborated with Nike, PacSun, Ermenegildo Zenga, and Kanye West. The Seventh collection also debuts a new Fear of God logo and includes a tribute to The Negro Leagues.

The Collection

The Seventh Collection is “rooted in easy elegance, our history and your reality” according to the brand. It is named after the number seven which symbolizes completeness and perfection and embodies that the brand’s “coming of age.” The collection was two years in the making and features a wide arrangement of pieces: Italian handmade tailored clothing, accessories, shoes, and basics.
