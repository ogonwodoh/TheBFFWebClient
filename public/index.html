Welcome to the Black Fashion Finder

The Black Fashion Finder is a permanent space for recognizing and celebrating Black contribution to fashion. Historically, Black people have made significant contributions to fashion, yet Black people are rarely credited for their work. There are only a few Black people that are credited for their work when they are popularized by celebrities or endorsed by white-owned fashion media. The Black Fashion Finder focuses on Black (co-)owned fashion labels. The mission is to increase the visibility of these fashion labels by featuring these labels on our app and writing about the innovative things these labels are doing.

Any black (co-)owned fashion label can be featured on the Black Fashion Finder. The Black Fashion Finder envisions a feature where everyone is included in fashion regardless of skin color, gender, class, nationality, ability, or sexuality.

You can download our app on the iOS App Store.
