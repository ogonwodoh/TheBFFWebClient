Welcome to the Black Fashion Finder

The Black Fashion Finder is a permanent space for recognizing and celebrating all Black contribution to fashion. Historically, the fashion industry has ignored and stolen the significant contributions to fashion that Black people have made. Only a few Black people are credited for their work when itâ€™s popularized by celebrities and/or endorsed by white-owned fashion media. The Black Fashion Finder wants to change this dynamic by recognizing the contributions of Black (co-)owned fashion labels. Our mission is to increase the visibility of these fashion labels by showcasing the work of these labels on our app and writing about the innovative things these labels are doing.

Any Black (co-)owned fashion label can be featured on the Black Fashion Finder. The Black Fashion Finder envisions a future where everyone is included in fashion regardless of skin color, gender, class, nationality, ability, or sexuality.


You can contact us at info@blackfashionfinder.com.

The Black Fashion Finder was created and built by Ogochukwu Nwodoh. Ogochukwu is a software engineer who is trying to tap more into her creative side.
