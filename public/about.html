About us

Our Mission

The Black Fashion Finder is a platform for understanding and promoting Black people’s position within fashion. On this platform, we will educate our audience about Black people’s contributions to fashion, explore the relationship between Blackness, fashion, and society, and promote the positionality of Black people in the fashion industry.

The Black Fashion Finder is owned by Ogochukwu Nwodoh.

Our Story

The Black Fashion Finder was created in the summer of 2020 by Ogochukwu Nwodoh. During the beginning of the COVID-19 pandemic, Ogochukwu started buying clothing from smaller clothing brands because she wanted to support them through the economic turmoil of the pandemic.
While she sought out smaller clothing brands, Ogochukwu started discovering Black-owned clothing brands. Ogochukwu fell in love with their creations and wanted to capture her discoveries somewhere. Before going on this journey, she was not aware that there were so many Black people in fashion creating innovative brands.
Therefore, Ogochukwu built the Black Fashion Finder to promote the current and past contributions of Black people to fashion. Ogochukwu wants to see a world where Black people receive more credit for their impact on fashion.
